Hello there! I’m a Senior Researcher at the Institute for Artificial Intelligence, University of Stuttgart, working with Prof. Dr. Steffen Staab on Graph Foundation Models, Geometric Deep Learning, Knowledge Graph Embedding and LLMs.

My research interests include Graph Foundation Models, Generative AI, Knowledge Graph Analysis, Large Language Models, Agentic AI, Geometric Deep Learning, and Semantic‑guided Deep Learning.

Feel free to reach out at mojtaba.nayyeri@ki.uni-stuttgart.de if you’d like to discuss ideas!

Updates

June 2023 – Present: Senior Researcher, Institute for Artificial Intelligence, University of Stuttgart (Advisor: Prof. Dr. Steffen Staab).
June 2021 – Present: Ph.D. in Computer Science, University of Stuttgart (Supervisor: Prof. Dr. Steffen Staab; Thesis: “Pattern‑aware Knowledge Graph Embeddings”).
Mar 2018 – Dec 2021: Researcher, Smart Data Analytics, Department of Computer Science, University of Bonn.
