Hello There! I’m an ELLIS and IMPRS‑IS PhD student advised by Steffen Staab and Antonio Vergari (University of Edinburgh). I’m generally interested in making ML systems learn better from data with inherent symmetries and where the i.i.d. assumption does not hold. My aim is to enhance the integration between Foundational Models (specifically LLMs) and Knowledge Graphs, exploring their mutual benefits and practical applications in real‑world scenarios.

Feel free to reach out to me through my email if you want to have a chat about ideas!
